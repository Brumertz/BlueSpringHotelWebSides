The Most Famous Things To Do In Cossack

Immerse Yourself in History and Culture

Cape Leveque & Dampier Peninsula: Embark on a guided tour or self-drive adventure to explore the rich history of the Dampier Peninsula. Visit iconic landmarks like the historic jetty and lighthouse in Cape Leveque, learn about the traditional custodians of the land, the Ngarluma people, and witness breathtaking Aboriginal rock art.

Experience the Extraordinary: Staircase to the Moon

Witness a natural phenomenon unlike any other â€“ the Staircase to the Moon. During certain full moon phases, an optical illusion creates the breathtaking spectacle of a shimmering staircase ascending from the tidal flats near Cossack. Combine this experience with a guided stargazing tour to learn about the constellations and immerse yourself in the vastness of the night sky.

Unwind and Embrace the Natural Beauty

Horseback Riding Adventure: Experience the stunning scenery of Cossack and surrounds on a guided horseback riding tour. Explore hidden beaches, meander through bushland trails, and witness the diverse flora and fauna of the region from a unique perspective. This activity is suitable for both experienced riders and beginners, offering a memorable way to connect with nature.
