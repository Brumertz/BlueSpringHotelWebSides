Cossack WA

Western Australia's (Bajinhurrba) Seaside Sanctuary Awaits

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating beauty of Cossack, Western Australia (Bajinhurrba). Nestled on the remote shores of the Dampier Peninsula, this former pearling boomtown offers a unique blend of rich history, stunning landscapes, and a deep connection to the Ngarluma Aboriginal people.

Cossack's vibrant past comes alive through the well-preserved historic buildings and the fascinating exhibits at the Cossack Museum. Learn about the town's role in the pearling industry, a vital part of Western Australia's maritime heritage.

Beyond the historical charm, Cossack boasts breathtaking natural beauty. Explore the rugged coastline, witness the diverse flora and fauna of the region, or simply relax and soak in the tranquility of this remote location.
